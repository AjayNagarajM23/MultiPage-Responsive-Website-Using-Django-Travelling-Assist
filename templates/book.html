Uttara Kannada district is one of the largest districts of the state and has abundant natural resources. The district with its distinct geographical features comprises of dense forest, perennial rivers, abundant flora and fauna and a long coastline. The district has 12 taluks namely Karwar, Ankola, Kumata, Bhatkala, Honnavar, Siddapur, Shirsi, Yallapur, Mundagoda, Supa (Zoida), Haliyala, Dandeli
